Welcome to Bergen

Bergen is a picturesque coastal city located on the southwestern coast of Norway, surrounded by mountains and fjords. It is known for its historic architecture, colorful wooden houses, and vibrant cultural scene.

Here are the top three things to do for a tourist visiting Bergen:

Explore the Fish Market - Bergen's famous Fish Market is a vibrant hub of activity where visitors can sample fresh seafood and other local delicacies. The market also offers a range of handicrafts, souvenirs, and other goods.

Take the Fløibanen Funicular - The Fløibanen funicular takes visitors up to the top of Mount Fløyen, providing panoramic views of Bergen and the surrounding fjords. At the top, visitors can enjoy hiking trails, restaurants, and cafes.

Visit Bryggen - Bryggen is the iconic harbor area of Bergen, known for its colorful wooden buildings dating back to the Hanseatic period. It is a UNESCO World Heritage site and a must-visit destination for tourists.
